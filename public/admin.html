QR Code Treasure Hunt Setup

How to Use These QR Codes

  1. Print all 5 QR codes (click "Print All QR Codes" below)
  2. Cut out each QR code
  3. Hide them according to the hints provided
  4. Start the hunt by sending your girlfriend to the first location
  5. When she scans each code, she'll get the next hint
  6. Make sure to hide your gift in the final location!
